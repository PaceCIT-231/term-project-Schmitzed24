Our Story

Pawesome Meals was founded with one simple goal in mind: to provide dogs with healthy, delicious, and nutritious meals that are tailored to their specific dietary needs. Our journey began when our founder, a lifelong dog lover, realized the importance of providing pets with the same quality nutrition we expect for ourselves. After years of research and collaboration with veterinarians, Pawesome Meals was born!

We believe that every pet deserves the best, and we’re dedicated to delivering top-quality meals made from only the freshest ingredients. From lean meats to vitamin-packed vegetables, our meals are crafted with love and care to support your dog’s health and happiness.

What sets us apart from other organizations is our commitment to transparency. Our option to build your own meal package allows for you to put your heart and mind into designing a package that works best for your best friend. Our meal package is streamlined to fit many dietary plans for dogs, and every kit built is reviewed by our team of veterinarians, to ensure that we both make the proper package for your loved one. It's always good to have a second set of eyes!

Our Mission

At Pawesome Meals, our mission is simple: to provide pets with high-quality, natural food that promotes health and well-being. We aim to change the way pet owners think about dog food. We are committed to sustainability, sourcing only locally grown produce and responsibly sourced meats. We don’t use artificial additives in our meals, to ensure that your dog gets the purest, most nutritious food possible.

Our meals are made with your dog’s unique needs in mind, and we offer customized meal plans that cater to different breeds, sizes, and dietary restrictions. Whether your pet needs weight management, joint support, or just a tasty treat, we have the perfect meal for them.
